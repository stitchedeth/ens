Skull by Preacher

The ten designs comprising the Skull by Preacher series were originally designed to be screenprinted onto fabric. The owner of each of the NFTs in this series is granted an exclusive copyright license to produce clothing featuring this artwork, subject to the Non-fungible Token Agreement, limited to a maximum of 1,000 clothing applications per year.
