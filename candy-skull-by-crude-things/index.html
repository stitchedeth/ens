Candy Skull by Crude Things

 The Candy Skull series by Crude Things was originally stenciled onto clothing by the artist's hand. The owner of each of the NFTs in this series is granted an exclusive copyright license to produce clothing featuring this artwork, subject to the Non-fungible Token Agreement, limited to a maximum of 1,000 clothing applications per year.
