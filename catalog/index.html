stitched.eth Garment Catalog

  • How to tell if a garment is authentic?
    • Step 1. Scan the NFC on the garment. The NFC should link to somewhere on this page with an image of the garment and a lot of other information, including the STX blockchain address. If the garment has a tye-dye tag, you can compare the photos of the tag on the garment with the one on this website and "eyeball" it to tell whether or not the garment is authentic. However, to establish final proof of authenticity, move on to step 2.
    • Step 2. Confirm that the STX NFT address listed on this website for the garment containing the NFC tag you just scanned is in the wallet of the person who is in possession of the garment. If you purchase a garment without getting the corresponding NFT, there will be no way for you to prove with absolute certainty that the garment is authentic. 
  • How does all of this work? As soon as the garment is manufactured, an NFT is created with a photo of the garment and the tag. This NFT will be transferred to the person who buys the garment. The owns the garment can prove authenticity of the garment by establishing they own the STX NFT Contract Address of Garment listed above. This is the only way to establish that the garment is authentic. If you are considering purchasing the garment, you must ensure that you receive both the garment and the corresponding NFT.
