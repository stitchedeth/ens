$THREAD Airdrop

You can obtain our governance token, called $THREAD, by participating in our ecosystem, attending events, and helping stitched.eth grow and thrive. You can vote your tokens on matters ranging from what artists are invited to where to allocation treasury funds. Learn more about how to obtain $THREAD tokens below.

What is the $THREAD token?

stitched.eth will be governed by holders of our $THREAD token. All of the $THREAD token holders will have the ability to vote, in proportion to the number of tokens they hold, on all governance matters, including which artists to invite, how to spend the treasury, and which art to mint into Design NFTs. 

How to get the $THREAD token?

stitched.eth does not sell $THREAD tokens, but $THREAD holders may of course transfer them since they have custody over the tokens - stitched.eth does not retain any control over the tokens once they're assigned. The only way to obtain tokens from stitched.eth is to "earn" them by participating in our ecosystem, attending events and accomplishing any of a number of tasks that the community needs from time to time. 

Here's what you need to do to get started:

  1. First, make sure you have a polygon wallet (it's free! if you're confused or having trouble doing this, reach out in our discord server and we'll help), and register for the airdrop here on our google form. All you *have* to do is to leave your polygon wallet address and a confirmation code, but if you plan on earning tokens by participating in IRL events, social media activity, or other means, you will want to share the relevant information on that form so that we know where to send your tokens for that participation. 
  2. Second, participate! There's a number of ways to earn $THREAD tokens right now. See the chart below for detailed information. The qualifying activities and specific reward amounts are subject to change at any time, and will certainly change as the community grows and changes. $THREAD holders will be able to vote on these changes.
  3. Third, once you've participated, we'll send you the $THREAD tokens. If you think we didn't send you what you earned, reach out to us on our discord server! We will be airdropping at various times.
